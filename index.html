My Vision

Technology changes fast, and website design trends are no different. Design elements and website features that were once modern and innovative may have become tired, overdone, and clinched in recent years. So here I am - combination of all classics and the new modern tech visions.

One of the most important web design standards is ultra-fast load time. Quick loading times have been essential factors in UI and SEO for years, and it continues to be a top priority for websites that want to rank well and convert better.

Micro Interactions and interactivity are one of the most important things for me. I also like to break up sections with fluid organic shapes or to use them in the background and behind products on their homepage, inspired by Android.

Flat Design and Thumb-Friendly Mobile Navigation are always two things with priority for me when I work on a projects.
